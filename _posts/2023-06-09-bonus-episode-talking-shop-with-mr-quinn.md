With our very first bonus episode we brought on one of the inspirations for the creation of the show, Mr. Quinn! Our beloved Mr. Quinn lives in Australia and is a huge fan of infinity even thought he does not get to play it as much as he would like. The boys talk about living in Aussie and the differences in the "meta" between America and Australia for a variety of games. They tell lots of bad jokes and have plenty of laughs along the way. Hope you enjoy!
